About Us

Welcome to Our Family

At Tadika Elit Ceria, we believe that the first steps in education should be filled with wonder. Located in the heart of Johor Bahru, our kindergarten is more than just a school—it's a vibrant community where children are encouraged to explore, create, and grow at their own pace. We combine structured learning with purposeful play to ensure every child develops a genuine love for discovery.

Established 2005

Our Journey

For over two decades, Tadika Elit Ceria has been a cornerstone of early childhood education in Johor Bahru. Founded in 2005, our journey began with a simple but powerful mission: to create a nurturing space where "Little Minds" could flourish into "Big Dreams."

2005

Opened our doors with a small, dedicated group of students and a vision for creative play.

2015

Expanded our facilities to include a dedicated Creative Arts wing and a modern, safety-first playground.

Today

Proudly nurturing over 100+ active explorers annually, preparing them for the challenges of primary school and beyond.

What We Believe In

Every child is a unique star. Our curriculum is designed to spark curiosity, foster emotional intelligence, and encourage bold self-expression through hands-on experiences.

Our Mission

  • To inspire growth, curiosity, and joy in every child.
  • To nurture young minds in a safe, playful, and caring environment.
  • To build a strong academic and social foundation for future success.

Our Vision

  • To shape confident, kind, and lifelong learners.
  • To create a world where every child feels valued and inspired.
  • To be Johor Bahru's most trusted space for early childhood discovery.
